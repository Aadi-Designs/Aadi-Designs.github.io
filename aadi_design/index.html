Pricing

I usually charge $50 per thumbnail. But the prices may vary depending on the style of the thumbnail (simple to complex).

We can negotiate the price once we agree on the thumbnail concept.

Turnaround Time

My usual turnaround time is 1-3 days. The exact turnaround time will be specified after we agree on the concept(s).

Feedback and Revisions

I don’t count the amount of feedback loops. Just let me know what you want tweaked, and we can work on it to finalize on a design that matches your vision.

If I believe your changes are straying too far from the original concept, I may offer you to pay for a second thumbnail.

A/B Testing

A/B testing can be achieved either by paying for more thumbnails (entirely different concepts) or for free by asking for exports of small variants (different text content, swapped colors, etc.) during feedback.

Communication

I prefer using Discord to communicate during our work process.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What's your step-by-step process when we work together?

    • 1. You send the video’s details and assets to me
    • 2. We come up with the concept together
    • 3. I specify the turnaround time and price for the amount of thumbnails
    • 4. I start designing and update you along the process
    • 5. I reveal a watermarked version
    • 6. I tweak it based on your feedback until finalized
    • 7. I send you an invoice request
    • 8. I deliver the finished thumbnail(s)

  2. How do you decide the concept?

    If you already have a clear concept in mind, I am open to hearing it before.

    Otherwise you can provide a basic concept of the thumbnail and also some example images of the style you prefer. I’ll then reveal my concept on which we can further discuss and finalize.

  3. What information/assets do you need to get started?

    I usually would need the details of your video (title, topic and description). I would also need your high quality images (if any).

    If you want a customized concept thumbnail, you can share the idea with me before taking the images so that I can guide you with the pose or the angle with which you can take your images.

  4. What happens if I'm not satisfied with the thumbnail?

    I always give a detailed concept before starting with the thumbnail design. I also keep you involved throughout the process to make sure we're on the right track. And if something’s not quite right, no worries — I offer revisions to get it just how you want it!

  5. How do you accept payments?

    I usually accept payments through PayPal and Wise.
